Services we offer

Donaldson Attorneys is a service-driven, results orientated firm. We provide 6 distinct service categories: General Litigation, Correspondent Services, Commercial Law, Family Law, Debt Collection, and Labour Law

GENERAL LITIGATION

All civil matters that require litigation in the High Court, Regional Court or Magistrate's Court, not limited but inclusive of the following:

  • Personal injury and delictual claims for losses and damages suffered, inclusive of Unlawful Arrest and Detention;
  • Criminal Law matters through the Magistrate court, Regional Court and High Court, inclusive of Legal Representations;
  • After-hours bail;
  • Specific performance and/or damages for breach of contract;
  • Motor vehicle collisions, inclusive of claims for excess and/or settlement quotations;
  • Evictions Applications through the High Court and Action proceedings through the Magistrate Court;
  • Arbitration and Mediation, inclusive of Rental Housing Tribunal;
  • Civil and Criminal law Appeals through the High Court, Supreme Court of Appeal and Constitutional Court.
CORRESPONDENT SERVICES

Assisting attorneys in running matters in Courts situated in areas beyond their jurisdiction, professionally and efficiently

  • Jurisdiction of the following Courts:
    • Johannesburg Labour Court
    • The Gauteng Local Division of the High Court (Johannesburg Central)
    • Randburg Magistrate’s Court
    • Roodepoort Magistrate’s Court
    • Krugersdorp Magistrate’s Court
    • Newlands Magistrate’s Court
    • Pretoria High Court
  • Processes, service and filing of documents and/or appearances;
  • Extensive and accomplished experience in civil litigation and in the areas of law pertaining to the National Credit Act and the application thereof;
  • Proficient in presenting Applications for consumers to be placed under Debt Review, as well as in opposing such Applications on behalf of Credit Providers;
  • Fees for all correspondent services are on the relevant party and party scale.
Commercial Law

Negotiation and drafting of all commercial contracts, inclusive but not limited to:

  • Purchase and supply agreements;
  • Company MOI's and shareholders' agreements;
  • Partnerships;
  • Commercial Leases;
  • Commercial dispute resolution (mediation, arbitration and litigation).
  • Applications.
Family Law

The following services, but not limited to:

  • Drafting and registration of antenuptial contracts;
  • Cohabitation Agreements;
  • Contested and Uncontested Divorces, inclusive but not limited to applications in terms of Rule 43 and Rule 58, respectively, for interim maintenance and Substituted Service and Edictal Citation;
  • Matrimonial dispute resolution;
  • Variation of Divorce Decree;
  • Maintenance Applications;
  • Proceedings relating to access to and custody of minor children, inclusive but not limited to parental plan agreements;
  • Guardianship Applications;
  • Proceedings through the Children’s Court;
  • Protection Order Applications.
  • Drafting of Last Will and Testaments.
  • Section 18 of the Immigration Act Relative's Visa Applications, inclusive of Appeals, Applications in terms of Promotion of Administrative Justice Act and Interdict Applications;
  • Section 26(b), (c) or (d) of the Immigration Act Permanent Residency Applications, inclusive of Appeals, Applications in terms of Promotion of Administrative Justice Act and Interdict Applications.
Debt Collection

Negotiations, drafting of agreements, mediation/arbitration, litigation, inclusive but not limited to:

  • Liaison with debtors;
  • Liaison with debt collectors;
  • Debt Collection;
  • Acknowledgement of Debts;
  • Summary Judgments;
  • Warrant of Executions, inclusive of legal representation for interpleader;
  • Emoluments attachment orders/ Garnishee orders;
  • Section 65A Notice to Appear
Labour Law

The following services, but not limited to:

  • Representation in the CCMA, Bargaining Council and Labour Court;
  • Independent chairing of disciplinary inquiries;
  • Employment guides;
  • Tailor-made employment contracts;
  • Client-specific policies;
  • Industry-specific disciplinary codes;
  • Drafting and issuing warnings;
  • Drafting and issuing of all formal employee notices;
  • Review of all disciplinary action;
  • Managing operational restructuring;
  • SLA management;
  • TES management;
  • LRA compliancy testing;
  • Review and optimisation of existing HR documentation;
  • Mutual interest dispute management,
  • Collective bargaining negotiations,
  • Labour compliancy certification,
  • Extensive and detailed employment handbooks,
  • Human resources training,
  • Specific disciplinary process training,
  • Managing dismissals for misconduct or incapacity,
  • Minimising trade union involvement,
  • Management of all CCMA/Bargaining Council documentation,
  • Separation of Conciliation and Arbitration processes at the CCMA/Bargaining Council,
  • Management and argument of all Condonation and Rescission Applications,
  • Training on CCMA/Bargaining Council litigation,
  • Comprehensive preparation of company witnesses for CCMA/Bargaining Council litigation,
  • Review of any adverse arbitration awards,
  • Applications to stay writs of execution,
  • Applications for strike interdicts, declarations, etc;
  • Appeals to the Labour Appeal Court, Supreme Court of Appeal and/or Constitutional Court
